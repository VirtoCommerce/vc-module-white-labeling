To enable personalized footer content for your organization through white labeling in Virto Commerce, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the Content section.

2. Choose the specific Store you want to customize the footer for.

3. Select 'Linked lists' from the widgets.

4. Create a new linked list by clicking 'Add'

5. Name this new list 'footer-{{ blade.organization.name }}' to identify it easily.

After completing these steps, your organization will start displaying the personalized footer content as intended.
